---

## 2. Cash on Delivery (COD)

### 2.1 Eligibility
- Available for pin codes where COD is supported.
- Maximum order value: Rs. 50,000.
- Not available for:
  - Kestral Plus members with COD returns above 20% in last 90 days.
  - Customers with 3 or more COD returns in the last 30 days.
  - Prepaid products (subscriptions, digital goods).

### 2.2 COD Convenience Fee
- Rs. 49 for orders under Rs. 1,000.
- Free for orders Rs. 1,000 and above.
- COD fee is non-refundable even if the order is returned or cancelled.

---

## 3. Payment Failures

### 3.1 If Amount Debited but Order Not Confirmed
- Amount is automatically refunded to the source within 5-7 business days.
- Customer does not need to contact support — refund is automatic.
- If refund not received after 7 days, customer should contact Kestral Support with transaction reference number.

### 3.2 If Payment Failed but Amount Not Debited
- Customer can retry payment from the Orders page.
- Order remains in "Payment Pending" state for 24 hours, after which it is automatically cancelled.

## 5. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds apply when:

1. **Missing items in return:** Deduction based on missing item value.
2. **Missing accessories:** Deduction as per accessory price list.
3. **Damaged packaging:** Rs. 100 deduction for electronics, Rs. 50 for other categories.
4. **Restocking fee:** 15% for opened electronics, Rs. 100 for clothing with missing tags.
5. **COD fee:** Rs. 49 non-refundable.
6. **Gift wrap charges:** Non-refundable.

---

## 6. Refund Disputes & Escalation

If refund amount is incorrect or not received:
1. Check refund status in Orders > Refund Tracker.
2. If status shows "Initiated" but not received after expected timeline, contact bank first.
3. If bank confirms no pending credit, contact Kestral Support with:
   - Order ID
   - Transaction reference number
   - Bank statement (last 10 days)
4. Kestral investigates within 48-72 hours.
5. If Kestral error confirmed: additional Rs. 100 wallet credit as goodwill.
