# Order Cancellation Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy ID:** KST-POL-002

---

## 1. Cancellation Windows

| Order Status | Can Cancel? | Refund |
|-------------|-------------|--------|
| Placed (within 30 minutes) | Yes, from app/website | Full refund |
| Placed (after 30 minutes, before shipped) | Yes, from app/website | Full refund |
| Shipped (in transit) | Yes, but cancellation request only | Refund after product returns to warehouse |
| Out for Delivery | No | Must refuse delivery or initiate return after delivery |
| Delivered | No | Must use Return process (see Returns & Refunds Policy) |

---

## 2. Automatic Cancellations by Kestral

Kestral may cancel orders automatically in these situations:

1. **Pricing errors:** If a product was listed at an incorrect price (e.g., Rs. 99 instead of Rs. 9,999).
2. **Stock unavailability:** Product went out of stock before warehouse processing.
3. **Payment failure:** Payment was not received within 24 hours.
4. **Suspicious activity:** Order flagged by fraud detection system.
5. **Delivery unserviceability:** Pin code became unserviceable after order placement.
6. **Customer request:** Customer contacted support for cancellation after self-cancel window closed.

---

## 3. Cancellation Charges

| Cancellation Stage | Charge |
|-------------------|--------|
| Before shipment | No charge |
| After shipment (in transit) | Rs. 99 deducted from refund as reverse logistics fee |
| Bulk orders (5+ items cancelled in 7 days) | Rs. 49 per item cancelled |

### 3.1 Exceptions to Cancellation Charges
- No cancellation fee if Kestral delayed shipping beyond the promised date.
- No cancellation fee if the wrong product was shipped.
- No cancellation fee for Kestral Plus (premium) members.

---

## 4. Refund for Cancelled Orders

Same refund methods and timelines as the Returns & Refunds Policy (KST-POL-001, Section 3).

- For pre-shipment cancellations: Refund initiated within 24 hours.
- For post-shipment cancellations: Refund initiated only after the product reaches the warehouse and passes inspection (typically 5-7 days after cancellation request).

---

## 5. Bulk Order Cancellation

- Orders with 5 or more units of the same product may be flagged as commercial/reseller activity.
- Such orders require manual verification and may be cancelled by Kestral.
- Repeated bulk cancellations may result in account restrictions.
