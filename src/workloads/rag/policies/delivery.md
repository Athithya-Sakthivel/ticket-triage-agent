# Delivery & Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy ID:** KST-POL-003

---

## 1. Delivery Timelines

| Region | Standard Delivery | Express Delivery | Same-Day Delivery |
|--------|------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad) | 2-4 business days | 1-2 business days | Available for select pin codes |
| Tier-2 cities (Jaipur, Lucknow, Indore, etc.) | 3-5 business days | 2-3 business days | Not available |
| Tier-3 cities & towns | 5-7 business days | 3-5 business days | Not available |
| Rural & remote areas | 7-12 business days | Not available | Not available |

### 1.1 Express Delivery
- Available for select pin codes and products marked "Express Eligible."
- Additional fee: Rs. 99 for orders under Rs. 999, free for orders above Rs. 999.
- Orders placed before 2 PM ship same day; after 2 PM ship next day.

### 1.2 Same-Day Delivery
- Available in select metro pin codes for orders placed before 11 AM.
- Product must be marked "Same-Day Eligible."
- Additional fee: Rs. 149 flat.
- Not available for large appliances, furniture, or items requiring installation.

---

## 2. Delivery Attempts

- Delivery partners will attempt delivery **3 times**.
- After the first failed attempt, customer receives SMS/email with reschedule link.
- After the second failed attempt, customer receives a final reminder with a 48-hour window.
- After the third failed attempt, the order is returned to Kestral warehouse and automatically cancelled.
- Refund for 3-attempt returns: Product amount minus Rs. 199 (forward + reverse shipping).

---

## 3. Delivery Delays

### 3.1 What Constitutes a Delay
- Standard delivery exceeding the promised timeline by 2+ business days.
- Express delivery exceeding the promised timeline by 1+ business day.

### 3.2 Compensation for Delays
| Delay Duration | Compensation |
|---------------|--------------|
| 1-3 days beyond promise | Rs. 50 Kestral Wallet credit |
| 4-7 days beyond promise | Rs. 100 Kestral Wallet credit |
| 8+ days beyond promise | Full refund of delivery charges + Rs. 200 wallet credit |
| Order not delivered in 21 days | Full refund + Rs. 500 wallet credit |

Compensation is automatic for eligible orders. No need to contact support.

---

## 4. Delivery Restrictions

- Kestral delivers to 25,000+ pin codes across India.
- Certain pin codes are unserviceable for large items (furniture, large appliances).
- Customers can check serviceability on the product page by entering their pin code.
- Kestral does not deliver to international addresses.
- Delivery to P.O. Box addresses is not available.

---

## 5. Open Box Delivery

For high-value electronics (phones, laptops, TVs above Rs. 20,000):
- Delivery partner will open the box in front of the customer.
- Customer must verify product condition, accessories, and physical damage.
- Customer provides OTP to delivery partner to confirm acceptance.
- If product is damaged, delivery partner takes it back immediately — no return process needed.
- Refusal during open box delivery = full refund, no return shipping charges.

---

## 6. Installation Services

- Available for large appliances (TVs, washing machines, ACs, refrigerators).
- Installation is scheduled separately from delivery (typically 24-48 hours after delivery).
- Installation is free for products marked "Free Installation."
- Customer must not open or install the product themselves — doing so voids installation warranty.
- If the installation team finds a manufacturing defect, they will initiate a replacement directly.
