# Grievance & Escalation Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy ID:** KST-POL-006

---

## 1. Grievance Definition

A grievance is any unresolved complaint regarding:
- Order not delivered despite confirmed payment.
- Wrong, damaged, or defective product received.
- Refund not received after promised timeline.
- Warranty or service claim denied without valid reason.
- Unprofessional behavior by delivery or service personnel.
- Account issues (hacked, blocked, unauthorized transactions).
- Any issue unresolved for more than 72 hours after first contacting support.

---

## 2. Grievance Levels

Kestral operates a 3-tier grievance escalation system:

### Level 1: Customer Support
- **Contact Methods:** In-app chat, email (support@kestral.in), phone (1800-123-4567).
- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours.
- **Resolution Time:** Within 48 hours of first contact.
- **Escalation Trigger:** If unresolved after 48 hours, automatically escalated to Level 2.

### Level 2: Senior Support Specialist
- **Assigned Automatically:** When Level 1 fails to resolve within 48 hours.
- **Contact Method:** Phone call from a dedicated specialist.
- **Resolution Time:** Within 24 hours of escalation.
- **Authority:** Can approve refunds up to Rs. 10,000, replacements, and wallet credits up to Rs. 500.
- **Escalation Trigger:** If unresolved after 24 hours or refund exceeds Rs. 10,000.

### Level 3: Grievance Officer (Nodal Officer)
- **Assigned When:** Level 2 unable to resolve or refund exceeds Level 2 authority.
- **Contact:** grievance.officer@kestral.in.
- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours.
- **Resolution Time:** Within 7 business days.
- **Authority:** Can approve full refunds, replacements, compensation up to Rs. 5,000, and policy exceptions.

---

## 3. Grievance Categories & Priority

| Priority | Category | Examples | Target Resolution |
|----------|---------|----------|-------------------|
| Critical | Financial harm, account security | Unauthorized transaction, account takeover, refund above Rs. 50,000 | 24 hours |
| High | Undelivered orders, defective high-value items | Order paid but not delivered, DOA phone, wrong product above Rs. 10,000 | 48 hours |
| Medium | Delayed refunds, warranty disputes | Refund not received after 7 days, service center rejected valid claim | 72 hours |
| Low | General complaints, service quality | Late delivery, rude behavior, minor product issues | 5 business days |

---

## 4. External Escalation (Consumer Forum)

If the grievance remains unresolved after Level 3:

1. Customer may file a complaint with the **National Consumer Helpline** at 1800-11-4000 or consumerhelpline.gov.in.
2. Customer may approach the **Consumer Disputes Redressal Forum** (Consumer Court) in their district.
3. For online transactions, complaints can be filed through the **Online Consumer Grievance Redressal Portal**.

Kestral commits to cooperating fully with all consumer forum inquiries.

---

## 5. Compensation for Grievance Handling Delays

| Delay Beyond Target | Automatic Compensation |
|--------------------|------------------------|
| Level 1 delay (beyond 48 hours) | Rs. 50 wallet credit |
| Level 2 delay (beyond 24 hours) | Rs. 100 wallet credit |
| Level 3 delay (beyond 7 business days) | Rs. 300 wallet credit + priority escalation |
| Total resolution exceeds 21 days | Full refund + Rs. 500 compensation |

Compensation is credited automatically. Customer does not need to request it.

---

## 6. Account Flagging & Review

To prevent misuse:
- Customers with 5+ grievances in 30 days may be flagged for account review.
- Fraudulent grievances (false claims of non-delivery, fake damage claims) will result in:
  - Warning on first instance.
  - Account restriction (COD disabled) on second instance.
  - Account termination on third instance.
- Kestral uses delivery partner geo-tagging, delivery photos, and OTP verification to validate claims.
