# Warranty & Service Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy ID:** KST-POL-004

---

## 1. Warranty Types

| Warranty Type | Duration | Coverage |
|--------------|----------|---------|
| Manufacturer Warranty | 12 months (standard), varies by product | Manufacturing defects only |
| Extended Warranty (Kestral Protect) | Additional 12-24 months | Extends manufacturer coverage |
| Accidental Damage Protection (ADP) | 12 months | Drops, spills, screen damage |

---

## 2. Manufacturer Warranty

### 2.1 Coverage
- Manufacturing defects that affect product functionality.
- Parts and labor costs covered.
- Does NOT cover:
  - Physical damage (drops, water damage, scratches).
  - Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications.
  - Normal wear and tear.
  - Consumables (batteries after 6 months, earphone tips, charger cables).
  - Software issues (unless caused by manufacturing defect).

### 2.2 Claim Process
1. Customer raises a service request via Kestral app or website.
2. Customer describes the issue and uploads photos/videos if required.
3. Kestral provides a service center address or arranges pickup (for large items).
4. Service center diagnoses the issue (typically 3-5 business days).
5. If covered: repair or replacement provided.
6. If not covered: customer receives a paid repair estimate.

---

## 3. Kestral Protect (Extended Warranty)

- Must be purchased within 30 days of product delivery.
- Extends manufacturer warranty by 12 or 24 months.
- Same coverage terms as manufacturer warranty.
- Transferable if product is sold (one-time transfer allowed).
- Claim process is identical to manufacturer warranty.

---

## 4. Accidental Damage Protection (ADP)

- Available for mobile phones, tablets, and laptops only.
- Must be purchased within 7 days of delivery.
- Covers:
  - Screen damage (cracked, shattered, display issues).
  - Liquid damage (spills, submersion).
  - Drops and impact damage.
- Limit: 2 claims per 12-month period.
- Service fee per claim: Rs. 499 for phones, Rs. 999 for laptops.
- Claim process:
  1. File claim via app with photos of damage.
  2. Kestral arranges pickup within 48 hours.
  3. Device repaired within 7-10 business days.
  4. If unrepairable: replacement with equivalent or better model.

---

## 5. Dead on Arrival (DOA)

- If a product is non-functional or damaged on first use:
  - Report within 48 hours of delivery for electronics.
  - Report within 7 days of delivery for all other products.
- Replacement or full refund provided.
- Customer must provide unboxing video or photos showing the defect.
- DOA claim processing: 48-72 hours.

---

## 6. Service Center Network

- Kestral has authorized service centers in 200+ cities.
- For pin codes without a service center, pickup and drop service is provided at no cost.
- Turnaround time: 7-14 business days depending on product and issue complexity.
- Loaner devices (temporary replacement) provided for premium customers for repairs exceeding 10 days.

---

## 7. Warranty Exclusions

1. Products with removed, altered, or unreadable serial numbers.
2. Products purchased from unauthorized resellers.
3. Products used for commercial purposes (unless marked "Commercial Grade").
4. Damage from natural disasters (flood, fire, earthquake).
5. Damage from power surges or use with incompatible accessories.
6. Products where the customer attempted self-repair.
