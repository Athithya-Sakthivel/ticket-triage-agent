# Returns & Refunds Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Policy ID:** KST-POL-001

---

## 1. General Return Window

Kestral offers a return window based on product category:

| Category | Return Window | Condition |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| Electronics & Mobile Phones | 10 days from delivery | Must be unopened, original seal intact |
| Clothing & Apparel | 30 days from delivery | Tags must be attached, unworn |
| Footwear | 30 days from delivery | Unworn, original box undamaged |
| Home & Kitchen | 15 days from delivery | Unused, original packaging |
| Books & Media | 7 days from delivery | Unopened, no damage to cover/packaging |
| Beauty & Personal Care | Non-returnable | Except if damaged or wrong item delivered |
| Groceries & Perishables | Non-returnable | No returns accepted for perishable items |
| Furniture | 10 days from delivery | Only if damaged during transit or manufacturing defect |

---

## 2. Return Conditions by Category

### 2.1 Electronics & Mobile Phones
- Product must be returned in original, sealed packaging.
- If the seal is broken, return is not eligible unless the product is Dead on Arrival (DOA).
- For DOA products, customer must record an unboxing video showing the defect.
- Serial number must match the invoice.
- All accessories (charger, cable, manuals, SIM ejector tool) must be included.

### 2.2 Clothing & Apparel
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags attached.
- Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
- Custom-stitched or personalized clothing items cannot be returned.
- Trial is allowed, but items with makeup stains, deodorant marks, or pet hair will be rejected.

### 2.3 Footwear
- Must be tried on a carpeted surface only.
- Soles must be clean and unscuffed.
- Original shoebox must be undamaged — do not use it as the shipping box.
- Return will be rejected if the shoebox has tape, labels, or writing directly on it.

### 2.4 Furniture
- Returns accepted only for:
  - Manufacturing defects (photos required).
  - Transit damage reported within 48 hours of delivery.
- Assembled furniture cannot be returned.
- Customer must retain original packaging for return pickup.

## 3. Refund Methods & Timelines

| Payment Method | Refund Method | Processing Time |
|---------------|---------------|-----------------|
| UPI / Net Banking | Back to source account | 3-5 business days |
| Credit Card | Back to card | 5-7 business days |
| Debit Card | Back to linked bank account | 5-7 business days |
| Cash on Delivery (COD) | NEFT to bank account | 5-7 business days after bank details verified |
| Kestral Wallet | Instant credit to wallet | Immediate |
| Gift Card | New gift card code issued | 1-2 business days |
| EMI (Card/No-Cost) | Back to card, EMI cancelled | 7-10 business days |

### 3.1 Cash on Delivery (COD) Refund Process
- Customer must provide bank account details (account number, IFSC code, account holder name).
- Bank account must be in the customer's name — third-party accounts not accepted.
- A Rs. 50 processing fee is deducted from COD refunds unless the return is due to Kestral's error (wrong/damaged item).

### 3.2 Wallet Refunds
- Wallet refunds are instant once the return is approved.
- Wallet balance can be used for future purchases or transferred to bank (transfer fee Rs. 10 applies).
- Wallet balance expires 12 months from last transaction.

---

## 4. Non-Returnable Scenarios

The following items and situations are not eligible for return:

1. Products marked "Non-Returnable" on the product page.
2. Products damaged due to customer misuse or mishandling.
3. Products missing serial numbers or UPC codes.
4. Products returned without original packaging or accessories.
5. Products where the return window has expired.
6. Free gifts, promotional items, or bundled accessories.
7. Digital products, software licenses, and downloadable content.
8. Products where the customer has initiated more than 3 returns in the last 30 days (account flagging for return abuse).

---

## 5. Pickup & Return Shipping

- Kestral offers free return pickup for most pin codes.
- For pin codes outside serviceable areas, customer must self-ship the product.
- Self-ship reimbursement: up to Rs. 200 for small items, Rs. 500 for large appliances.
- Pickup will be attempted twice. If customer is unavailable for both attempts, return request is cancelled.

---

## 6. Refund Disputes

If the refund amount differs from the expected amount:
1. Check if any shipping charges, COD fee, or platform fee were non-refundable.
2. Check if a restocking fee applies (15% for opened electronics, Rs. 100 for clothing with missing tags).
3. Contact Kestral Support with order ID for investigation.
4. Dispute resolution timeline: 48-72 hours.
